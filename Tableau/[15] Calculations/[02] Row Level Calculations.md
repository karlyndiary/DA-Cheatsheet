### Number Functions

- Ceiling: Rounds up the number. Ceiling(1.8) => 2
  - Ceiling([Sales])
- Floor: Rounds down the number. Floor(1.8) => 1
  - Floor([Sales])
- Round: Round the number to its nearest integer. Round(1.8) => 2
  - Round([Sales]), Round([Sales], 2)
